Ideal Breakfast

Description

The most delicious filling breakfast, you'll never get sick of it!

Ingredients

  • pasteured eggs
  • chorizo
  • avocado
  • cheese

Steps

  1. heat up a pan with a small amount of avocado oil
  2. add chorizo to pan and break up into small pieces
  3. once chorizo has browned, break eggs into the pan
  4. mix it all up together
  5. add to avocado toast and sprinkle cheese on top
